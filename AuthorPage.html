Jax Haper: Next in Line Game Changer Nameless Love
Jax Harper, a shy and soon-to-be hero, has just lost his mother and is having crazy dreams. Little does he know, these dreams will drag him into a world he has never known, yet is destined to save. When aspiring video game designer Kyra Burke meets an unexpected online companion, her life is turned upside down. You will love this modern spin on a classic love story! When Twenty-Two year old, Cassie Lane, drunkenly decides to take a vacation alone, she meets and eventually falls for a bartender. They agree that this is strictly a vacationship and they should not share each other's names in order to avoid reuniting after she returns home.
